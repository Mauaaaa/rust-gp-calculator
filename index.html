Defense & Electricity

Quick setup guides for common defense circuits.

Smart Alarm Setup
  • Place Smart Alarm on wall.
  • Connect Power In (Requires 1 rW).
  • Connect Power Out to a switch or HF Sensor.
  • Hold 'E' on Alarm to pair with Rust+ App.
Auto Turret (3-Chain)
  • Requires 31 rW Total Power.
  • Battery -> Switch -> Splitter (x3 Outputs).
  • Connect Splitter outputs to 3 Turrets (10 rW each).
  • Important: Fill turrets with ammo before turning on.
